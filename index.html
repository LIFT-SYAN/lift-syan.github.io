About

The LIFT Lab conducts research in rotorcraft aerodynamics, aircraft icing, advanced aerial mobility, and high-performance computational fluid dynamics.

Research

  • Rotorcraft aerodynamics and aeroacoustics
  • Aircraft and engine icing
  • Advanced Aerial Mobility (AAM)
  • GPU-accelerated CFD and Lattice Boltzmann methods
